Donate

This section of the website is dedicated to supporting the Ordo Draconis server.
At the moment, the donation system is still being prepared. It will not be a pay-to-win system.
The entire system is currently under development, as our goal is to create a server where donations
do not affect gameplay or provide unfair advantages. At this time, we are only considering
a possible rate increase for VIP players. However, nothing has been decided yet, and it is still unclear
whether it will be possible to purchase cosmetic items such as mount skins or mounts themselves.
Seeing Lineage II from a saddle will not affect the game in any way.
Donations are therefore completely optional.
We will be grateful for any contribution that helps support the operation of the server.
