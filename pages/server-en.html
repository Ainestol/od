Introductory speech of the Ordo Draconis clan

Once, the world of MMORPGs was more than just a game to us. It was a second home.

Sometime around 2004–2005, we discovered the power and beauty of an online world that was able to unite people from different parts of the country — and the world — into one whole. It was back then that friendships, rivalries, alliances… and clans with a soul were formed. One of them was Ordo Draconis.

We lived through the era of C4, C5, C6. Long nights, endless farming, our first raid bosses, the tension of PvP, the joy of victory and the bitterness of defeat. We did not play alone — allies stood by our side in alliances, people with whom we shared raid bosses, wars, and ordinary moments in cities.

We started on the IceOnline server. And then came a moment that is never forgotten.

An unannounced wipe. A decision made by a single admin that instantly erased weeks and months of effort. It left a scar on the entire community — one many of us still carry to this day. Not because of lost characters — but because something truly special was destroyed without warning.

This server was created as a tribute to those times.
A tribute to everyone who was part of Ordo Draconis.
A tribute to those who stood with us in alliances.
A tribute to those who raided with us, fought by our side — or against us.

This is not a return in time.
It is a chance to remember.

The server is set up to be enjoyable to play, to respect the players’ time, and to allow the atmosphere of the old good days to be experienced once more — without unnecessary pressure, without frustration, with a strong focus on community.

If you played here…
If you remember those names, those moments, those nights…
Then this world is for you as well.
