Privacy

Ordo Draconis processes only data necessary to operate the website, the game portal, and to keep the service secure. The goal is not data collection — it is a functional and safe server.

What data we process

  • Email – registration and login to your web account.
  • Technical data (e.g. IP address, user-agent, timestamp) – security, audit, abuse prevention, incident handling.
  • Game identifiers (e.g. game account / character) – only for portal features (VIP, wallet, vote) and ownership verification.

Why we process it

  • account management and authentication (session)
  • security (rate limiting, abuse detection, incident logs)
  • portal features (VIP, wallet, vote) and correct account assignment

Emails and marketing

We do not send marketing emails. Your email may be used only for operational purposes (e.g. registration/login, security notices, account-related communication), if such features are enabled on the website.

Cookies / session

The website uses essential technical cookies for login and system operation (e.g. session). These cookies are necessary for the website to function.

Sharing and access

We do not sell data to third parties. Access is limited to authorized administrators and only as needed for operations and incident resolution.

Retention and deletion

Account data is kept while the account exists. Upon request, the web account and related web data can be deleted. Security logs may be retained for as long as necessary to protect the service and handle incidents.

Your rights

  • request access to your data and corrections
  • request account deletion and web data removal (unless logs must be kept for security reasons)
  • object to processing based on legitimate interests

If you want your account deleted or have privacy questions, contact us via the ticket system (recommended) or Discord.
